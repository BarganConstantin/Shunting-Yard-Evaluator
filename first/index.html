Our Story

When you send a cake, you aren’t just sending dessert. You are sending a moment of celebration, joy, and togetherness. When you can’t be there to celebrate in person, We Take The Cake makes it easy to delight your family, friends and colleagues with our made from scratch cake deliveries; just like grandma made with whole buttermilk, fresh eggs and real butter.
